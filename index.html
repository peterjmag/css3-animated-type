About

This little distraction is brought to you by Peter Magenheimer (who really needs to finish his portfolio site). This is pretty much pure HTML and CSS3, with a small splash of jQuery to randomize the starting positions of the blocks and to handle keyboard input. The awesome CSS3 transition property handles most of the heavy lifting.

Credits

The concept was inspired in part by David Desandro's Curtis CSS Typeface, and by his other experiments with CSS3 and animation.

I adapted the typeface itself from a pixel font called 04b_03.
